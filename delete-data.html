Privacy Policy

You can delete your data stored by TrackIron using the options below.

1. Delete data on your device (instant)

Most TrackIron data stays on your phone only. To remove it:

  1. Open Settings on your Android device.
  2. Go to Apps → TrackIron.
  3. Tap Storage → Clear data (or Clear storage).

Alternatively, uninstall the app. This removes local workout logs, nutrition entries, profile, and preferences.

2. Request deletion of server data

If you used optional online features (for example, a personal record submitted to the leaderboard), a limited amount of data may be stored on the developer’s server (such as display name, exercise name, weight, reps, and date).

To request deletion of that server-side data, email the developer with:

  • Subject line: TrackIron data deletion request
  • Your display name as shown in the app (if applicable)
  • Approximate date(s) of any leaderboard / PR submissions you want removed

Email:

shrikant.hiwase@gmail.com

I will respond and delete matching server records within a reasonable time (typically within 30 days).

For privacy questions, see the Privacy Policy or email shrikant.hiwase@gmail.com.
