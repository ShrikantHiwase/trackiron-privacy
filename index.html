1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TrackIron (“we”, “the app”, “I”) handles information when you use this Android app. TrackIron is published by Shrikant Deokrishna Hiwase, an individual developer (not a company). I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about my practices.

Google Play requirement: This policy is provided to meet Google Play’s disclosure requirements and to help you understand how I treat personal and sensitive data.

2. Information the app processes

2.1 Data stored on your device (local)

Most of your information stays on your device in local storage (including a local database). This may include:

  • Profile and fitness data: name, weight, height, age, gender, activity level, calorie goals, and related preferences.
  • Workout data: exercises logged, sets, reps, weights, durations, session summaries, personal records, and progress metrics.
  • Nutrition data: foods and meals you add, servings, and calorie/macronutrient information you enter.
  • App preferences: customized workout plans, weekly plans, hydration reminder settings, and similar choices you make in the app.

This local data is not transmitted to me by default solely because you use these features; it remains on your device unless you use optional online features described below.

2.2 Account sign-in (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, the app uses Google’s sign-in flow. Google’s handling of your account is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. I may receive identifiers such as your email address and name as provided by Google to create or restore your account experience. I use this to associate your profile with your account and, where applicable, to sync profile-related data with an optional backend as described below.

If you use the app as a guest without Google sign-in, account identifiers from Google are not used.

2.3 Optional online services (leaderboard / profile sync)

If enabled in the app’s configuration, the app may communicate with a remote service (for example, to display a leaderboard or to store a copy of certain profile fields tied to your signed-in email). When used, transmissions may include, for example:

  • Profile fields you have provided (such as name, email, and fitness-related profile inputs).
  • Personal record (PR) submissions you trigger by logging workouts (such as exercise name, weight, reps, estimated one-rep max, and date).

If these services are not configured or not used, such data is not sent to servers I control.

2.4 Notifications

The app may schedule local notifications on your device (for example, hydration reminders). These are delivered by the operating system’s notification system; I do not use them to collect the content of your notifications on any server I control.

2.5 Device data

I do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the app to track you across other companies’ apps or websites. Standard device and OS information may be processed by Google Play and Google as part of installing and updating the app, per their policies.

3. How I use information

I use the information described above to:

  • Provide core app features (logging workouts, nutrition, progress, and customization).
  • Operate optional sign-in and, where applicable, leaderboard or profile sync.
  • Improve reliability and fix issues (for example, error information you choose to send me, if any).

I do not sell your personal information.

4. Legal bases (where applicable)

If laws such as the GDPR apply, I rely on appropriate bases such as performance of a contract (providing the app), consent (where required, e.g. optional features), and legitimate interests (operating and securing the service), as applicable.

5. Sharing and retention

  • Third-party services: As an individual developer, I may use services such as Google’s tools or simple hosting for optional features (e.g. leaderboard). Those providers process data only as needed to run those services, under their own terms and policies.
  • Legal: I may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
  • Retention: Data on your device remains until you delete the app or clear app data. Data on optional servers, if any, is kept only as long as needed for those features unless a longer period is required by law.

6. Security

I use reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; I cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data, or to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact me using the email at the top of this policy. You can also delete local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

8. Children’s privacy

TrackIron is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe I have done so, please contact me and I will take steps to delete such information.

9. International users

If you use the app from outside the country where I live, your information may be processed in countries that may have different data protection laws.
